    1. Hash Rate: 11.5TH/s 5%

    2. Power Consumption: 1275W + 7% (at the wall, with APW3 ,93% efficiency, 25C ambient temp)

    3. Power Efficiency: 0.098 J/GH + 7%(at the wall, with APW3 93% efficiency, 25C ambient temp)

    1. Power consumption figures will vary with your PSU’s efficiency, the ambient operating temperature and the accuracy of the power meter.
